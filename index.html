Pat's Salmon Cookies

Pat forged her famous cookie cutters in the fires of Mt. Doom, imparting a certain hobbitish characteristic that's sure to energize your long walks in the Shire.

If there's one thing Pat loves more than Salmon Cookies, it would be her weekly acid trips. This artwork was inspired by an exceptionally radical trip she had while following the Grateful Dead in the mid 1970's.

Nothing says love like Pat's patent and DEA pending MDMA frosting. It's guaranteed to have you loving everyone with arms reach.

We're not sure who this family is. Pat just really likes this stock photo from a Prozac flier she got in the mail. We think it reminds her of the life she could have had if she hadn't broken up with her high school love Mark... Kind'a makes you wonder..., whatever happened to Mark?
